Where Tradition Meets Innovation

At Sakura & Salt, we honor the centuries-old traditions of Japanese cuisine while embracing modern culinary innovation. Every dish tells a story of craftsmanship, precision, and passion.

Our chefs trained in Kyoto and Tokyo bring authentic techniques to create an unforgettable dining experience. We source the finest seasonal ingredients, from Hokkaido sea urchin to Wagyu beef from Kobe.
